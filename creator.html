Creator Bio

Leia Kook-Chun is a multidisciplinary creative exploring the possibilities of art, design, and technology. Curious to learn, she studies various topics to grow her knowledge and build a solid understanding of perspectives to inform her work and approach to solving problems. Her exploratory approach to media has led to using a combination of media for projects to enhance the aesthetic and the experience of the work. With respect to [Project Title], her creative code practice typically involves interaction as a key element to boost viewer engagement and enjoyability. Her artistic practice uses art as a mode of expression and to build connection with others. Her design practice works to solve problems with a myriad of frameworks and approaches.
She recently completed the Digital Futures program at OCAD University, receiving a Bachelor’s of Design. Throughout her academic career, she delved into code, game design, computer science & theory, physical computing, visual history, multimedia, web design, data analytics & visualization, user experience design, and research. In her elective courses, she learnt about art for social change, ethics, advertising, economics, genomics, culture & communications, innovation, sociology, science fiction, and sustainability.
