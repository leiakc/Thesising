Clear

Draped, oil ridden paint
Curls off the once clean and pristine kitchen walls
To reveal
Tapestry summer blooming flowers,
Watercolour-faded,
Uncovered,
Under appreciated.
The ping pong ball keeps bouncing,
But not pouncing
The player sweating tick, tick, tick
Waiting to hear the pick, pick, pick
Of the ball
To choose a side –
A decision.
Thoughts jumble your mind
Crazed and scattered
Messy
